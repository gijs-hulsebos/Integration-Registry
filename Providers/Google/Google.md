## 💪 Core Strengths

### 1. Infrastructure Supremacy
Google owns the full stack. From custom **TPU v6 chips** to global data centers, they offer massive compute efficiency for enterprise-scale AI.

### 2. Native Multimodality
Unlike models that "stitch" vision and text together, **Gemini** was built to be natively multimodal from day one—handling video, audio, and code in one go.

### 3. The Search Advantage
Direct access to the Google Search index allows for high-accuracy "grounding," meaning fewer hallucinations and more up-to-date facts.
