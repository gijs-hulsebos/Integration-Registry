# 📓 Google NotebookLM

> **An AI research and writing assistant powered by your own data.** NotebookLM uses the Gemini Pro 1.5 model to ground responses exclusively in the documents you provide, creating a "source-grounded" AI experience.

---

## 💡 Key Capabilities
- **Source-Grounded Reasoning:** Unlike standard LLMs, it prioritizes your uploaded PDFs, Google Docs, and websites to minimize hallucinations.
- **Audio Overviews:** Generates a "Deep Dive" podcast-style conversation between two AI hosts discussing your uploaded material.
- **Auto-Summarization:** Instantly creates study guides, FAQs, and table of contents from complex research papers.
